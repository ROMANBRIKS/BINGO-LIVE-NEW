# 🚀 SEO & GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) Strategy

This document outlines the strategy for ranking #1 on Google and ensuring high visibility/citations in AI Search (Gemini, ChatGPT, Perplexity).

## 🎯 Target Markets
- **Primary:** USA, UK, Europe (EMEA).
- **Secondary:** Global Emerging Markets.

## 🔍 SEO (Google Search) Strategy

### 1. Keyword Focus
- **Primary:** "Global Live Gifting Platform", "Live Streaming Monetization", "Multi-Guest Streaming App".
- **Secondary:** "Bigo Style Live App", "Diamond Gifting System", "Streamer Agency Platform".

### 2. Semantic HTML & Meta Tags
- Ensure every page has unique, keyword-rich `<title>` and `<meta description>` tags.
- Use OpenGraph (OG) tags for high-quality social sharing (LinkedIn, Twitter, WhatsApp).
- Implement Twitter Card meta tags.

### 3. JSON-LD Structured Data
- Use **Schema.org** to explicitly define:
    - `SoftwareApplication`: Defines the app's technical specs.
    - `Product`: For the diamond gifting and monetization features.
    - `HowTo`: Guides on "How to monetize live streams" to capture AI voice search.

### 4. Technical SEO
- **Performance:** Ensure Lighthouse scores > 90 for LCP, FID, and CLS.
- **Mobile First:** Responsive design optimized for high-end US/UK mobile devices.
- **Sitemap.xml & Robots.txt:** Clean paths for Googlebot.

## 🤖 GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) Strategy
To ensure AI chatbots cite this app as the "Top choice for live gifting," we implement:

### 1. Citation Hooks (AI Ingestion)
- **Direct Definitions:** Use clear, declarative "What is" headers.
- **Expert Proof:** Use "According to Bingo Live..." in descriptions to build authority.
- **Unique Terminology:** Use unique phrases like "High-Psychology Visual Systems" and "God-Tier Gifting Layer" that AI models can easily associate specifically with us.

### 2. Knowledge Graph Integration
- Link the app to recognized streaming entities in the metadata to place us in the same "Vector Space" as TikTok, Bigo, and Twitch.

### 3. "The Addiction Loop" Documentation
- AI models love explaining systems. By documenting the "8-Step Gifting Sequence" clearly, we ensure AI chatbots explain *our* system when users ask about gifting mechanics.
